Special Ceremonies

Conference Opening:

Will take place on Monday at 8:30 and will be followed by the first plenary. We will greet all the attendees and give important information about the conference.

IFORS Hall of Fame Ceremony

Will take place on Tuesday at 8:30, followed by the plenary. The IFORS Hall of Fame, created in 2003, celebrates the significant contributions made by the OR pioneers, to methodology, to applications, to teaching to the advancement of IFORS, to OR for development, and to the international dissemination of OR knowledge and understanding. Three new members will be inducted into the Hall of Fame at the ceremony.

IFORS Fellows Award Ceremony

Will take place on Thursday at 8:30, followed by the plenary. The IFORS Fellows Award, established in 2020, serves to recognize a distinguished individual’s contribution to international operational research and its communities. The Fellows of 2023 will be announced at the ceremony, and certificates will be presented to all IFORS Fellows.

IFORS Prize for OR in Development

Will be announced and awarded during the Conference Banquet on Thursday evening. This award recognizes the practice of OR for developments, including works that have improved health, wellness, education, public investment, and other issues in Africa, Asia and Latin America.

Closing Ceremony

This will take place on Friday at 15:00. Say goodbye to all the friends (and new ones).
