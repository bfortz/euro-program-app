Information

Venues:

Don't forget that the conference will take place at two different venues, located within walking distance from each other. The schedule has, however, been designed to reduce moving from one place to the other. The two venues are:

  • The “Centro de Extensión” of Pontifical Catholic University of Chile. In what follows we refer to this venue as “CE” and to Catholic University as PUC.

  • The School of Business of the University of Chile. In what follows we refer to this venue as “FEN” and to the University of Chile as UChile.

Conference check-in:

Will be located at Centro de Extensión (CE) and will begin on Sunday at 14:00 and will remain open Monday to Thursday from 8:00 to 17:00 and Friday to 13:00.

Exhibit Hall

It is located at FEN, the Business School of University of Chile. It will be open from Monday to Thursday.

Coffee and lunch:

At lunchtime on Monday, Tuesday, Thursday, and Friday, we will provide a complimentary box lunch, so you don't have to worry about finding a place to eat elsewhere. This box lunch will be available at the Hall of the Business School of the University of Chile (FEN), from 13:15-14:15, Monday, Tuesday, and Thursday, and from 12:45 to 13:45 on Friday.

There will also be complimentary coffee breaks during each day of the conference.

Wifi:

If you have Eduroam at your institution, you should be able to connect at both venues. There will be other Wi-Fi networks available, and the login instructions will be available at check in. Complimentary Wi-Fi is available at the venues, which will also work for those who have Eduroam at their institution. Further information about this will be sent later, along with tips on using your cell phone in Chile.

Information desk:

There will be information desks in both venues. Staff there will guide you around and can also give you general information. There will also be a special desk for tourist information.
