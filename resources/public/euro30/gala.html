Gala Dinner

EURO 2019 Delegates attending the Gala Dinner can be assured of an unforgettable evening of excellent food, fine wine, and an evening of cultural entertainment that only Dublin can offer!

The Gala Dinner will take place on Tuesday 25th June at 20.00 in the home of Irish Rugby and Irish Football, Aviva Stadium. Aviva Stadium sits on the grounds of the oldest sports stadium in Europe. It was founded by Henry Dunlop in 1872 as a multi- purpose athletics grounds. Six years later, in 1878 the first international rugby match was played between Ireland and England which also makes it the oldest rugby international test venue in the world.

Tickets must have been pre-purchased and will not be available onsite at the conference.

How to get there?

Shuttle buses will be collecting from four locations. Places on the buses have been pre-booked. Buses will collect from UCD, Radisson Blu St Helens hotel, Talbot hotel and Nassau st at 19.00.
