Welcome Party

Céad Míle Fáilte! We look forward to welcoming you to EURO 2019.

University College Dublin will host the welcome party after the opening session on Sunday 23 June 2019, 17:30-19:00. Please join us from 19.00 to 21.00 for a Welcome party in O’Reilly Hall and O’Brien Centre for Science, to include a drinks reception. Registration will be open from 14.00 in the O’Brien Centre for Science for you to collect your delegate badge and bag.

How to get there?

The UCD campus in Belfield is served by a variety of bus services. From the city centre at D'Olier Street. The journey time is approximately 30 minutes. The 39a bus terminates inside the UCD campus and the 46a, 145 and 155 buses stop outside the main entrance to the campus
